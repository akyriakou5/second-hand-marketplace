Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how our Second-Hand Marketplace application collects, uses, and protects user data. This project is created for educational purposes.

1. Information We Collect

When users register or use the platform, we may collect the following information:

  • Username
  • Email address
  • Password (stored securely)
  • Product details (title, description, category, price)
  • Images uploaded by the seller

2. How We Use Your Information

The collected data is used only for the basic functionality of the application, including:

  • Creating and managing user accounts
  • Allowing sellers to upload and manage products
  • Displaying product listings to users
  • Allowing visitors to contact sellers through email

3. Data Storage

User data is stored in a local MySQL database. Passwords are securely hashed and are not stored in plain text.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share personal data with third parties. Some seller information, such as username and email address, may be shown within the application so visitors can identify and contact the seller.

5. User Responsibility

Users are responsible for the information they provide, including product descriptions and contact details. Please avoid sharing sensitive personal information.

6. Security

We take basic security measures such as password hashing to protect user data. However, this project is for educational use and not intended for production use.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as the project evolves.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer.
